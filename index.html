Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TinyCrmGo

Is TinyCrmGo really only $1.99 per month? +
Yes! Our Starter plan is $1.99 per month and includes full access to all features. You only pay more when you need more users or customer capacity.
Are all features included in every plan? +
Absolutely. All plans include the same complete feature set—lead management, customer management, invoice and order management, products, team collaboration, AI-enhanced features, reports, and audit logs. Pricing only varies by the number of users and customers you can manage.
How quickly can I get started? +
You can be up and running in minutes. Simply sign up, verify your email, and start adding your leads and customers. No complex setup or training required.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later? +
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at your next billing cycle. No penalties or fees.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security measures including secure authentication, encrypted data transmission, regular backups, and GDPR-compliant data handling. Your data is safe and under your control.
Does TinyCrmGo work on mobile devices? +
Yes! TinyCrmGo is fully responsive and works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. Access your CRM from anywhere, on any device with an internet connection.
What kind of support do you offer? +
All plans include email support. Pro and Business plans include priority support with faster response times. We're committed to helping you succeed.
